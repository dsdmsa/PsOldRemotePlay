    Are we talking about PlayStation3 Remote Play or PlayStation4?

    PlayStation3 Remote Play its only available for Windows Platform as far as i am aware, but the quality will leave a sour taste in your mouth, compatibility isn't great either, not too far from the PSP or PSVITA, even with CFW installed, streaming fails over the WIFI, and localy don't even what to know, youre juat better of buying thoes cheap Chinese recorders (EasyCap).

    On the PlayStation4 Side, Remote Play its only available for High-End Sony Xperia Models starting from the 2014 Xperia Z2, depending on the device the experience may vary, but usually the best for Remote Play its from Xperia XZ onwards, Xperia Z4 and Z5 with the Snapdragon 810 its an Overheating Hog, which impacts streaming performance (the Xperia i regret owning), and the Z2 and Z3 h don't support 5Ghz WIFI bands on the SoC, but of course you can get Remote Play working on other devices, but its a hassle, and not all functions work such as DUALSHOCK4 Pairing, i tested myself when i owned a OnePlus 3T after the Z4, but ive heard that iOS also got Remote Play available with DUALSHOCK4 support.
